TGS/Lottie info:
 - TGS files are gzip'ed Lottie json files
 - https://mattia.basaglia.gitlab.io/python-lottie/group__Lottie.html
 - https://github.com/airbnb/lottie-android/tree/master/lottie/src/main/java/com/airbnb/lottie
 - Telegram only supports a subset of Lottie animations, see:
   https://core.telegram.org/animated_stickers

Play Lottie animations with html/js:
 - https://github.com/airbnb/lottie-web

Gzip can be decoded with pako:
 - https://github.com/nodeca/pako
